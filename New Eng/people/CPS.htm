Research

Tumor metastasis is a key step in facilitating the progression of human cancer. We mainly focus on investigating the mechanism of how cancer cells acquire the capacity to metastasize and the possible therapeutic strategies. Our research interest is identification of the tumor suppressive/oncogenic factors, including proteins and non-coding RNAs.
